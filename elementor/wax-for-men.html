The Process

How It
Works

Every visit follows our structured protocol to ensure consistency, safety, and the best possible results.

01

Consultation

We review your medical history, previous waxing experience, and any skin sensitivities. First-timers get a thorough walkthrough of what to expect.

02

Contraindications Check

A quick screening ensures your skin is ready for treatment. We check for sunburn, medications, recent procedures, and any conditions that could affect results.

03

Preparation

The treatment area is cleaned and prepped with pre-wax oil. We select the right wax system for your skin and hair type — hot wax, strip wax, or combination.

04

Treatment

Precise, efficient waxing with expert technique. We work methodically to minimise discomfort and maximise clean results.

05

Aftercare

Post-treatment products are applied and you receive detailed aftercare instructions covering the first 48 hours and ongoing maintenance.
