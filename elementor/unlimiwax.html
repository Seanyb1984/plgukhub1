FAQ

Common
Questions

Is this a contract or a subscription?

It’s a fixed-term course of treatments, financed over 24 months. Think of it like buying in bulk at a wholesale price — you know exactly what you’re getting and exactly what you’ll pay. It’s not a rolling subscription and there are no surprise renewals.

Will checking my eligibility affect my credit score?

No. We use a soft credit check to assess eligibility. This is invisible to other lenders and has zero impact on your credit score. It takes about 60 seconds and you’ll get an instant decision.

What does 0% APR actually mean?

It means you pay no interest whatsoever. The total you pay over 24 months is the same as the course price. There are no hidden fees, arrangement charges, or early repayment penalties.

What if I need to cancel?

You have a 14-day cooling-off period after signing. After that, the course is a fixed commitment. If you do need to cancel, any refund is calculated as: Total Finance Amount minus (sessions used x full PAYG price). We also offer a 3-month grace period for rescheduling missed sessions.

How is this regulated?

UnlimiWax finance is provided through an FCA-authorised lender. We operate as a Secondary Credit Broker, meaning we introduce you to the finance provider but don’t lend directly. All advertising complies with FCA CONC 3 requirements.

Can I add more areas later?

Yes. You can add additional areas at any point by starting a new plan for those areas. Your existing plan continues unchanged.
