Clinical Standards

Safety & Clinical Governance

In an unregulated industry, our status as a premier male aesthetics clinic is defined by our commitment to clinical safety. We believe that aesthetic medicine should be held to the same rigorous standards as any other healthcare field. Every practitioner is trained in advanced complication management.

Our consultation process is distinct. Before any treatment plan is designed, we conduct a thorough medical and aesthetic assessment. This ensures that every procedure we recommend is appropriate for your specific physiology and aligned with your personal goals.

Our approach is rooted in the science of male facial morphology. Men possess thicker skin, higher bone density, and different muscle distribution than women. By focusing exclusively on these differences, we avoid the “feminised” results often seen in generalist clinics.

We strictly follow the guidelines set out by the Joint Council for Cosmetic Practitioners (JCCP) to ensure patient wellbeing remains our primary focus.
